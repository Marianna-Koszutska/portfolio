Alien Emergence

About the Project

Alien Emergence is a short cartoon-art game I developed solo, from concept and visuals to code and sounds. You play as an alien who finds themself stranded on Earth.

With only your impeccable disguise and a slightly questionable understanding of human culture, you must navigate unfamiliar streets to avoid suspicion from always-on-the-watch grandmas.

Designed as a quick and charming experience, the game combines minimalist mechanics with expressive visuals to explore.

Game Features

  • Memory/reflex mini-game
  • Mysterious "alien-walk"
  • Time-based
  • Immersive animations
  • 100% Solo Dev: Coded, drawn, designed, and imagined entirely by me
