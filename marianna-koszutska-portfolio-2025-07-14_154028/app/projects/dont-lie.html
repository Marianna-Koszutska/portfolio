(Don't) Lie

About the Project

A story-driven crime game about lying your way through a police interrogation. Created in just 12 hours during a BerlinMiniJam by an incredible team of 7 game designers.

Game Features

  • Unique lie-detection mini-game
  • Intriguing nonlinear crime narrative
  • Mysterious, noir-inspired atmosphere
  • Interactive dialogue with multiple outcomes

My Responsibilities

Narrative Design

  • Part of a team of two to write character dialogue and branching outcomes
  • Designed the interrogation structure with lie detection logic
  • Created tension-driven plot with layered character motives
  • Integrated clues and contradictions into player choices
