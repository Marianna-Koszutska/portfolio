The Museum of Berlin: The Ancient City

About the Project

The Museum of Berlin: The Ancient City is a narrative-driven, text-and-image-based game set in a distant future, where players explore a virtual museum dedicated to the long-lost metropolis of Berlin. Through a series of humorous, haunting, and thought-provoking exhibits, players uncover the city's contradictory legacy: a sanctuary of radical creativity, freedom, and nightlife—but also a site of urban decay, displacement, and forgotten dreams.

The experience combines satire and melancholy to reflect on Berlin’s cultural soul, questioning what remains when rebellion gets archived. Heavily inspired by Douglas Addams’s style of writing.

Game Features

  • Futuristic Museum Interface
  • Nonlinear Exploration
  • Speculative Fiction narrative
  • Photographic Storytelling
