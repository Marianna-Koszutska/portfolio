Mindset: The Forgotten Story

About the Project

Mindset: The Forgotten Story is a narrative-driven VR puzzle game where players explore a fragmented mind and uncover a forgotten story through environmental exploration, puzzles, and interactive dialogue.

Game Features

  • Immersive VR puzzle experience
  • Exploration of a fragmented mental landscape
  • Environmental storytelling and interactive puzzles
  • Narrative progression through player discovery
  • Atmospheric world-building focused on memory and identity

My Responsibilities

Programming

  • Implemented gameplay mechanics and interactive elements
  • Worked with Unity systems to bring design ideas into the game
  • Supported integration of puzzles and player interactions

Level Design

  • Designed puzzle flow and player progression
  • Created spaces that supported exploration and discovery
  • Balanced gameplay challenges with narrative pacing

Narrative & UI Design

  • Developed narrative elements and environmental storytelling
  • Designed UI elements to support player interaction
  • Collaborated on creating a cohesive player experience
